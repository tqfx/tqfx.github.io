generate for cubic polynomial trajectory

\begin{aligned} \left\{\begin{array}{l} t=t_{1}-t_{0}\\ q=q_{1}-q_{0}\\ k_{0}=q_{0}\\ k_{1}=v_{0}\\ k_{2}=\cfrac{\left(-2\,v_{0}-v_{1}\right)\,t+3\,q}{t^2}\\ k_{3}=\cfrac{\left(v_{0}+v_{1}\right)\,t-2\,q}{t^3} \end{array}\right. \end{aligned}
